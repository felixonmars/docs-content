Scaleway's Serverless Jobs allow you to run recurring tasks in the cloud. A Job is composed of a job definition, and some job runs.

This page explains how to create a job definition, execute it and delete it.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) 
  - You have [signed up to the private beta](https://www.scaleway.com/en/betas/#serverless-jobs) and received a confirmation email.
</Message>

## How to create a job definition

You can create a job either from the Scaleway Container Registry or any other public container registry (Docker Hub, AWS Container registries, GitLab container registry, etc.).

### From the Scaleway Container Registry

1. Click **Jobs** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The Jobs page displays.

2. Click **+ Create job**.
